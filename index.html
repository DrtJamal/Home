Dr. Tauseef Jamal

About Me

I am an Associate Professor at the South East Technological University (SETU) Ireland with more than 32 years of teaching and research experience at various educational and research institutions.

I hold a PhD in Telecommunications from the University of Minho, University of Porto, and University of Aveiro, Portugal, completed in 2013. I also have an MS in Computer System Engineering from Halmstad University, Sweden, earned in 2006.

Research Interests

My research interests include:

  • Cyber Security and Forensics
  • Body Area Networks and E-health
  • Big Data and Cloud Security
  • Wireless Ad-hoc and Sensor Networks
  • Internet of Things (IoT)
  • Computational Intelligence and Machine Learning

Professional Experience

I have been affiliated with renowned institutions including:

  • CC Lab, Halmstad University, Sweden (2004-2006)
  • LMN Lab, Polytechnic University Bucharest, Romania (2008)
  • IAN Unit, INESC Porto, Portugal (2008-2010)
  • University Lusofona, Portugal (2010-2014)
  • COPELABS, LIG Lab, France (2017)
  • University Nova Lisboa, Portugal/IT Lisboa (2018)
  • University of Mons, Belgium (2019)
  • PIEAS University Islamabad, Pakistan (2020)
  • University Politechnique Catalunya (UPC) Barcelona, Spain (2023)
  • National College of Ireland (NCI) Dublin, Ireland (2024)

My contributions include RelaySpot in cooperative WLANs, focusing on relay-based MAC layer design, performance modeling, and protocol enhancements. I have actively participated in EU-level research proposals, project meetings, and academic activities for the MS IT program at PIEAS.

Current Role

Currently, I am an Associate Professor at SETU, Department of Computing, and serve as the DCIS Project Coordinator. I am also the coordinator of CoCoLab and a visiting researcher at multiple international universities.
